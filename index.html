About Todd Alström

Hey, I'm Todd Alström. Founder of BeerAdvocate. Living in Helsinki, Finland.

Dad (human & cats), husband, OG champion of beer, social organizer, coder, privacy & tech enthusiast, coffee fiend, sci-fi geek, punk, gamer, Knight of the Brewers' Mash Staff, American Brewers Guild graduate, veteran, and whatnot.

Most of my beer (olut) drinking is Finnish lagers these days. Also obsessed with ruisleipä, lakristi, salmiakki, friluftsliv, and kalsarikännit since moving to Suomi.
